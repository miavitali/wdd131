Site Plan: Bairespel

1. Site Name & Logo

Site Name: Bairespel

Bairespel is a paper product company. The name reflects its Argentine roots ("Baires") and specialization in paper products ("pel").

Logo Concept: A clean, minimalist logo using a soft pink and light blue palette, possibly including a paper roll or abstract product icon.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of the Bairespel website is to present its line of paper products for both home and industrial use. Visitors will learn about the company, explore the catalog of products (like toilet paper, napkins, cleaning rolls, and interleaved towels), and easily find contact information and business location.

3. Scenarios

  • "What types of industrial cleaning rolls does Bairespel offer and in what sizes?"
  • "Where is Bairespel located, and how can I contact them for a wholesale order?"
  • "Can I see images and specifications for each product (napkins, toilet paper, etc.)?"

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #AEEEEE (Light Blue) - background color and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #e762b0 (Pink) - buttons, highlights, headers.
  • Text Color: #333333 - general text color for readability.
  • Background: #FFFFFF (White) - content background areas.

5. Typography

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - modern and clean.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - readable and friendly.

6. Wireframe (Home Page Layout)

The website navigation is responsive:

  • On desktop: the navigation bar displays links to Home, About Us, Products, and Contact.
  • On mobile: a hamburger menu icon is shown. When tapped, it reveals these navigation links in a dropdown.
